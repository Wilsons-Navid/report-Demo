ScamScan

The problem

Fraud now arrives
as a message.

SMS & mobile money

A fake M-Pesa / MoMo alert asks for a PIN or an OTP. The money moves in seconds, and rarely comes back.

The wallet is the account

In low-resource markets a mobile wallet holds the savings. A drained wallet is not an inconvenience.

Filters miss it

Generic spam filters learn from Western email. Code-mixed, region-specific scams slip straight through.

02
ScamScan

The solution

It doesn't just flag a scam. It tells you what to do.

ScamScan reads the message text alone, then turns the verdict into action. That action layer is the differentiator.

01 — READ

The risk

A calibrated verdict across four fraud classes, with a colour-coded risk level and a plain-language read of what the message wants.

02 — ACT

What to do now

Class-specific safety steps. Never share a PIN or OTP. Do not pay a fee for a prize. Verify through an official channel.

03 — REPORT

Who to contact

Real reporting contacts for the user's region. Kenya 333 / KE-CIRT, Nigeria EFCC / 7726, Ghana CSA 292, and more.

03
ScamScan

Taxonomy

One verdict, four classes.

Phishing

Credential and link harvesting that impersonates a bank, telecom or service.

Mobile-Money Fraud

Wallet, PIN, agent and OTP cons targeting M-Pesa, MoMo and similar.

Advance-Fee Fraud

Prize, lottery, 419 and inheritance lures that ask for an up-front fee.

Not a Scam

Ordinary, benign messages. The residual class that keeps precision honest.

The approach

From corpus to live verdict.

A deliberately classical pipeline: interpretable, robust on a small corpus, and cheap to serve.
